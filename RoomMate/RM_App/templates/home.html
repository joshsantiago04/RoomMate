What is RoomMate?

RoomMate is a roommate matching services created by Josh Santiago, Gaynay Doo, and Cayden Calo. RoomMate seeks to streamline the roommate search by offering an easy, hassle-free platform for people to use. Create a profile, select your living preferences, and get matched with people who won't make you pull out your hair in frustration. Sign up now!
